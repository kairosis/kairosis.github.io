## What is Kairosis?

Kairosis is a self-hosted platform that connects to external tools via webhooks, polling, local agents, and device integrations — normalizes every signal into a common `NormalizedEvent` envelope — and publishes it to a RabbitMQ topic exchange.

**One job: collect, normalize, publish.**
